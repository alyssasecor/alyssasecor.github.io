ABOUT

My name is Alyssa Secor. I am a graphic designer in Northwest Indiana. My education has given me knowledge of both graphic design and marketing. I use this knowledge to work with my clients and create unique and professional designs.

WHAT I DO:

LOGOS:

Logos are one of the most important aspects of a companies image. They are the first impression a customer gets of a company. I help my clients design logos that make that first impression a good impression.

BRANDING:

Brochures, flyers, business cards, banners, and other branding materials are vitally important to a companies success. Creating consiting branding through all of these devices is something I strive to do.

PHOTOGRAPHY:

Unique, high quality images of your company or products can be used in many different ways. I aim to take photographs that capture my clients personality.
